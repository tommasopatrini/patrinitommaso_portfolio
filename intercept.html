Sexting Coercion / Video Intercept / 2024

Developed within the We Say Stop campaign at Politecnico di Milano, this project focuses on raising awareness of gender-based violence and discrimination through the specific lens of sexting coercion. The research phase involved producing a video intercept to investigate how students perceive and understand the issue, gathering insights through interviews with around eighty participants.

The video adopts a guerrilla-style approach, combining on-site interviews with expressive portrait photography to document authentic reactions and emotions. Through its visual and narrative construction, the project contributes to a deeper understanding of psychological pressure in digital spaces and supports broader communication efforts promoting consent and respect.
