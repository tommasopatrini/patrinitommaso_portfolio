Let me in / Music Videoclip / 2025

Directed for the second time for emerging artist Camilla Buzzetti, student at CPM, Let me in is a country-pop-inspired anthem of resilience and emotional release. The video visually translates the frustration and strength behind the urge to be heard, reclaim power, and start anew. Produced by Alta Vibe and recorded at Alari Park Studios.
