Milan Design Week / Reportage / 2025

Milan Design Week is one of the world’s leading events dedicated to design and innovation. Each year, alongside the Salone del Mobile, the Fuorisalone transforms Milan into a dynamic stage for exhibitions, installations, and new creative expressions.

This project presents a photographic reportage from the 2025 edition, capturing the energy, experimentation, and visual diversity that define the city during this unique week of design culture.
