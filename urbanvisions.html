Urban Visions / Editorial Book / 2023

Urban Visions is an editorial project exploring the legacy of civic design and public utility graphics, with a focus on the work of Albe Steiner, Massimo Dolcini, and the ISIA Urbino tradition. The research investigates how social design principles evolved from mid-20th-century civic graphics into contemporary visual languages, linking historical content and artifacts with present-day communication systems.

The project culminates in a prototype for a three-dimensional installation and a publication that reinterprets Steiner’s and Dolcini’s approaches through modern editorial design. Drawing inspiration from functional Swiss aesthetics and urban signage, Urban Visions examines the dialogue between visual form, public space, and the social role of the designer.
