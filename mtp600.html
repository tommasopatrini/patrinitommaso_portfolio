MTP600 / Editorial Design / 2025

MTP600 is a typographic editorial project conceived as a brand extension proposal for CEME, a company specializing in technical pumps and valves. The publication reinterprets industrial communication by transforming the MTP600 peripheral pump into a graphic and narrative object, revealing the aesthetic potential of mechanical design.

Featuring a brutalist, monochromatic layout, the project translates technical data into a bold visual language through typography, threshold effects, and rhythmic composition. Designed as part of a collectible editorial series, MTP600 introduces a new narrative tool for the company, one that celebrates the technical legacy of industrial production from a contemporary and expressive perspective.
