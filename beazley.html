Beazley / Corporate Event / 2025

Beazley is a global insurance company that organizes curated events in iconic locations worldwide. For the annual Milan edition, the event took place on the rooftop terrace of Maio Restaurant, overlooking the Duomo. The project involved the complete production of the official aftermovie, encompassing filming, editing, color grading, and post-production to capture the atmosphere and identity of the occasion.
