Ageusia / Short Film / 2024

Directing a short film inspired by Martin Scorsese for the "Ricette d'autore" theme was one of the most challenging yet exciting projects I’ve worked on. The goal was to reinterpret Scorsese’s cinematic style while integrating the preparation of a culinary dish. I oversaw everything from script development to directing, working closely with actors, the cameraman, and the team to bring this unique concept to life. I also handled the video editing during post-production and contributed to the visual identity of YellowCab Production.

The film received awards for Best Editing, Best Film, and Best Direction among other class projects, highlighting the dedication and collaboration behind the project.
