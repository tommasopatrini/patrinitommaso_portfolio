Ageusia / Short Film / 2024

Ageusia is a short film inspired by the cinematic language of Martin Scorsese, particularly Taxi Driver and The King of Comedy. The project reinterprets his visual and narrative codes to explore ambition, alienation, and obsession within the world of haute cuisine, translating urban tension into an intimate psychological conflic

The work covered all stages of film production, from concept development and screenplay writing to shooting, editing, and post-production. It also included the creation of the fictional studio YellowCab Production, designed with a complete visual identity and animated logo. The film received recognition among class projects, earning awards for Best Editing, Best Film, and Best Direction.
