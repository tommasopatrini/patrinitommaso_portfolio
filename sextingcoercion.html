Sexting Coercion / Social Campaign / 2025

As part of the We Say Stop campaign about gender-based violence, this project addresses the hidden issue of Sexting Coercion, shedding light on a term often misunderstood or ignored. Targeting the student community at Politecnico di Milano, the initiative challenges the normalization of manipulation and pressured consent in digital spaces, reinforcing the right to say no without guilt, fear, or blame.

By fostering awareness and respect for personal boundaries, the campaign creates space for reflection and dialogue. It empowers students to recognize coercion, resist manipulation, and advocate for healthy, consensual interactions in both digital and intimate relationships.
